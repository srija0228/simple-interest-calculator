# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge
We pledge to make participation respectful and harassment-free for everyone.

## Our Standards
- Be respectful
- Use inclusive language
- Be supportive of others

## Enforcement Responsibilities
Maintainers are responsible for enforcing this code.

## Scope
Applies to all project spaces and public interactions.

## Enforcement
Violations may be reported and will be reviewed.

## Enforcement Guidelines
Appropriate action will be taken based on behavior.
